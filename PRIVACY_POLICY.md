# Privacy Policy for Floating Ghost Image

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

## Overview

Floating Ghost Image ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Information Collection and Use

**The App does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or user data.**

### What We DON'T Do:
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your usage or behavior
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT require internet connection
- We do NOT access your network or transmit data
- We do NOT share any information with third parties
- We do NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- We do NOT request storage or media access permissions

### Permissions Used:

The App requests only one permission for its core functionality:

**Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)**:
- Required to display the floating overlay image on top of other apps
- This is the core functionality of the app

### Image Selection:

The App uses Android's native photo picker to select images:
- No storage or media permissions are required
- The system handles image selection securely
- Images are processed locally on your device
- No images are uploaded, stored externally, or shared
- Selected images remain in their original location on your device

All data processing happens locally on your device. No data ever leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages as it operates entirely offline without any data collection.

## Data Storage

Images you select remain on your device in their original location. The App only stores your image selection preference locally on your device. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or external libraries that collect data.
